  • About

  • I’m the person in the garage you drive past late at night with the lights still on. You probably wonder, “ What’s he doing in the garage at this time of night? “ The answer is that I am probably working on my furniture. This is what I enjoy doing and am passionate about.

    My love for furniture has developed from my interest in making things with my hands. I enjoy fine craftsmanship, quality materials, attention to detail and the beauty of using materials in a way that may surprise. I love the challenge of designing a new furniture piece, creating a sense of art while keeping functional integrity.

  • Coming from an interior design background the Valorous Range is a venture into a series of pieces that incorporate the same design style. All pieces within the Valorous range can be modified to suit customer requirements. In fact you may have an idea for a piece that I haven’t thought of yet, so let’s talk and see if we can design a piece to suit your needs.

    Hopefully I have now answered your question as to why that shed light was on so late at night!

    My workshop is my shed, just a simple shed in the suburbs of Wellington. Thanks so much for taking an interest in my designs.

    Craig.
