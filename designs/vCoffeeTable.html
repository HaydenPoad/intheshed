  • Valorous Coffee Table

  • This Valorous Coffee Table features two drawers. The drawers are fitted to two way drawer runners, making them accessible from both sides of the table. As with other Valorous designs, the bendy plywood top wraps around the dresser and continues downwards to form legs, suspending the drawers above the ground. Horizontal recesses with a finger holds enable easy pull-out, replacing traditional drawer handles.

    Materials: 18mm Birch Plywood drawer fronts. Laminated 3mm Bendy plywood form the 'wrap around' edging, top and sides. 12 mm Plywood forms the construction of the Drawers. Colours on the table shown are Resene 'Pitch Black’ and Resene ‘Red Pepper’.

    Dimensions are 900mm wide, 450mm high, and 600mm deep. They can be tailored to suit.
