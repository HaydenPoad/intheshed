  • Valorous Desk

  • As with the Valorous range, this unit is wrapped in Bendy Plywood that continues downwards to form legs. Underneath the top surface sits a single drawer on the right. A finger hold is recessed into the bottom of the drawer leaving a clean Birch Plywood drawer front.

    King Slide Soft Close Drawer Runners are used.

    Materials of Desk shown: Birch Plywood (18mm door front) Laminated 3mm Bendy Plywood ('wrap' around edging and facing surfaces) Coloured with Resene wood stain and finished with Resene satin polyurethane.

    Colours of Desk shown: Natural Birch Plywood and Resene Pitch Black.

    As with my other designs all colours within the Resene interior wood stain range are available.

    Dimensions can be tailored to suit.
