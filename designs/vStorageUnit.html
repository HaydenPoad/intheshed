  • Valorous Storage Unit

  • The 1st Valorous Storage Unit shown measures 1700mm wide, 900mm high and 450mm deep. Coloured with Resene ‘Pitch Black; wood stain and finished with Resene polyurethane. On this unit the cabinet doors are stained with Resene ‘Rock Salt’ (white) before being Polyurethaned. Adjustable shelves are positioned behind the doors.

    The 2nd unit shown features three drawers on the left, and a cabinet door on the right. Adjustable shelves sit behind the cabinet door.

    Materials: 18mm Birch Plywood drawer and cabinet door fronts. Laminated 3mm Bendy plywood form the 'wrap around' edging, top and sides. 12 mm Plywood forms the construction of the Drawers. The unit is coloured with Resene 'Satin Orange' wood stain and a satin polyurethane finish.

    Dimensions 1500mm wide, 750mm high, 450mm deep (dimensions can be changed to suit)
