  • Retro Entertainment Unit

  • $1950.00

  • This unit is a development of my previous designs. In this design I wanted to incorporate colour and the use of different materials in interesting ways. The use of Birch Plywood and laminated Bendy Plywood is consistent with my Valorous Furniture Range but I have added interest by using transparent Acrylic sheet for the sliding doors. Acrylic sheet has also been used in the design of the door handles.

    The unit features six separate shelving areas, four of which are closed by transparent Acrylic sliding doors.

    This unit can be modified to suit customer requirements.

    All colours within the Resene interior wood stain range are available.

    The unit pictured measures 1600mm wide, 450mm deep, and 570mm high.
