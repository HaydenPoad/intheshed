  • Valorous Music Unit

  • As with the Valorous range, the Valorous Music Centre is wrapped in Bendy Plywood that continues downwards to form legs. A horizontal recess with a finger hold enables easy pull-out of the drawer.

    This piece was designed to enable the record player to sit on top, cd's to fit in the drawer, amp to sit on the shelve and the records to stand in the vertical shelves.

    This design was finished in Resene Pohutukawa semi gloss. All colours within the Resene interior wood stain range are available.

    King Slide Soft Close Drawer Runners are used for the drawers.

    Materials: Birch Plywood (18mm drawer front and horizontal shelve, 9mm vertical dividers) Laminated 3mm Bendy Plywood ('wrap' around edging and facing surfaces)

    Dimensions can be tailored to suit.

    Please contact for pricing.
