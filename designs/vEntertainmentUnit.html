  • Valorous Entertainment Unit

  • Valorous Entertainment Units can be customed designed to suit your needs. A variety of drawer and shelve positions are possible. As with the all the Valorous designs the bendy plywood top wraps around the unit and continues downwards to form legs, suspending the drawers above the ground. Horizontal recesses with a finger holds enable easy pull-out, replace traditional drawer handles.

    King Slide Soft Close Drawer Runners are used.

    Materials: 18mm Birch Plywood drawer fronts and shelves.
    Laminated 3mm Bendy plywood form the 'wrap around' edging, top and sides. 12 mm Plywood forms the construction of the Drawers.

    Dimensions can be tailored to suit.

    Please contact for pricing.
