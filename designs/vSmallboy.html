  • Valorous Smallboy

  • $1150.00

  • The Valorous Smallboy features two drawers. As with the Valorous bedroom furniture range the bendy plywood top wraps around and continues downwards to form legs, suspending the drawers above the ground. Horizontal recesses with a finger holds enable easy pull-out, replace traditional drawer handles.

    King Slide Soft Close Drawer Runners are used.

    Materials: 18mm Birch Plywood drawer fronts. Laminated 3mm Bendy plywood form the 'wrap around' edging, top and sides. 12 mm Plywood forms the construction of the Drawers.

    All colours within the Resene interior wood stain range are available.

    Standard Smallboy dimensions: 600mm wide by 540mm high by 445mm deep.

    Dimensions can be tailored to suit.
