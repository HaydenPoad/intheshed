  • Valorous Bookcase

    Colours shown are Resene ' Pitch Black' and Resene 'Desert Yellow" Wood Stain. All surfaces are finished with Satin Polyurethane.

    All colours within the Resene interior wood stain range are available.

    Dimensions can be tailored to suit.
