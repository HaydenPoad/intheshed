  • Valorous Longboy

  • $2150.00

  • The Valorous Longboy features three drawers, which are internally divided into two divisions. As with the Tall boy the bendy plywood top wraps around the dresser and continues downwards to form legs, suspending the drawers above the ground. Horizontal recesses with a finger holds enable easy pull-out, replace traditional drawer handles.

    Materials: 18mm Birch Plywood drawer fronts. Laminated 3mm Bendy plywood form the 'wrap around' edging, top and sides. 12 mm Plywood forms the construction of the Drawers.

    King Slide Soft Close Drawer Runners are used.

    All colours within the Resene interior wood stain range are available.

    Dimensions of standard Valorous Longboy. 1400mm wide, 805mm high, 445mm deep. Dimensions can be tailored to suit.
