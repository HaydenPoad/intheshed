  • Valorous Tallboy

  • $2150.00

  • The Valorous Tallboy features four large drawers and two smaller top drawers which are wrapped in bendy ply that continues downwards to form legs, suspending the drawers above the ground. Horizontal recesses with finger holds enable easy pull-out, replacing traditional drawer handles.

    Materials: 18mm Birch Plywood drawer fronts. Laminated 3mm Bendy plywood form the 'wrap around' edging, top and sides. 12 mm Plywood forms the construction of the drawers.

    King Slide Soft Close Drawer Runners are used.

    All colours within the Resene interior wood stain range are available.

    Standard Tallboy dimensions: 890mm wide by 1140mm high by 445mm deep.

    Dimensions can be tailored to suit.
