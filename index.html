  • Furniture

  • It started with a Smallboy and an opportunity at a local Wellington design store. Years later, working with many fabulous and creative clients, I've enjoyed the challenge of adapting the Valorous style to include many different designs. Being a creative person, I am always keen to explore other designs and styles. Every piece of furniture I build is an opportunity for me to learn and develop new skills. Thanks so much for taking an interest in my designs.

  • About

  • Hi everybody, thanks for visiting my website! My woodworking journey started as many small Kiwi businesses do... In the shed! With a background in interior design and model making, I'm a typical hands-on guy. I'm always satisfied to stand back at the end of the day and say "I built that."

    I hope to grow and continue in my woodworking journey. Maybe there won't be any room in the shed for a while longer.

    Craig.
